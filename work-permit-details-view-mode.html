                  1. The ADA Permit to Work is only applicable to the area specified by the Requestor.
                  2. Requestor/Project Manager must send official notification to stakeholders prior to work commencement.
                  3. The PTW Requestor must display the approved ADA Permit to Work in a conspicuous location and keep it available at the site(s), including all required NOCs for audit and inspection.
                  4. The PTW Requestor must comply with all applicable safety regulations set by ADA, ADOSH, GCAA, Aviation Security Regulations, and other relevant Abu Dhabi SRA.
                  5. The PTW Requestor is responsible for securing all required passes for their materials, personnel, and equipment.
                  6. The PTW Requestor shall ensure that all designs are approved by the ADA Relevant Department and Fire Safety Department for any building/land-use changes within any ADA owned facilities/premises before PTW submission. This PTW process does not include approval of the designs.
                  7. The PTW Requestor shall ensure that ADA Relevant Department and Fire Safety Department approve all materials to be used for any modification within ADA owned facilities/premises before PTW submission. This PTW process does not include approval of the materials.
                  8. The ADA EHS Delegated Department must obtain all required approvals/NOCs as per ADA EHS PTW Procedure prior to commencement of the planned activities.
                  9. The PTW Requestor must submit all requirements to the ADA EHS Delegated Department to obtain ADA FSS Isolation Permit approval prior to any Airport FLS system isolation/interruption.
                  10. The PTW Requestor must ensure site safety arrangements are implemented prior to and during execution of the planned activities.
                  11. The PTW Requestor must ensure proper housekeeping at all times; all waste/FOD generated during the activities shall be disposed of accordingly.
                  12. The PTW Requestor must reinstate, replace, or repair any airport asset(s) damaged during execution of the activities.
                  13. The PTW Requestor shall ensure a valid Permit to Work is maintained during the work; no activities may commence after the permit has expired.
                  14. The PTW Requestor/Project Manager is responsible for keeping all certificates valid for the duration of the PTW.
                  15. For locations requiring keys or access cards, the request must be processed per the Key Management procedure.
                  16. The PTW Requestor is responsible for notifying the ADA EHS Delegated Department Representative 3 weeks prior to permit expiry for extension/renewal.
                  17. For PTW extension/renewal, the contract number, scope of work, and locations must reflect the previously approved PTW.
                  18. Any non-compliance with the approved Method Statement, Risk Assessment, or any unsafe act observed by ADA EHS/ADA FM/ADA EHS Delegated Department/ADA stakeholders or representatives may result in suspension or cancellation of the PTW.
                  19. If the contractor fails to comply with the conditions of the approved PTW and submitted documents, AD Airports reserves the right to suspend the works and take further action against the contractor and project manager.
                  20. This PTW does not absolve the project team of responsibility to ensure works are carried out in accordance with regulatory requirements, applicable codes, and airport procedures.
                  21. This permit is subject to cancellation at any time if the bearer/contractor does not follow the terms and conditions.
                  22. The PTW Requestor/Project Manager must notify the ADA EHS Delegated Department Representative once activities are completed. The PTW will be closed only upon confirmation from the ADA Relevant Department.
